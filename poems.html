Feature Poem:

Maybe It's True

I am no Super-Hero
I am full of flaws
Someone once said;
`it takes two years to know a person'
Maybe it's true
But it's such a long time
To keep something as beautiful as love on hold
To keep a beautiful woman waiting
To keep a guy truly in love anxious
Maybe time is the only true test character
Maybe time we prove the verity of all things

I am no Super-Man
Finite of days is my flaw
Someone once said;
'mortality is our only curse and blessing'
Maybe it's true
Then today is all we have,
We can never be more beautiful than we are today
We can never be happier than we are today
Because any moment can be our last

I am not Super-Rich
Limitation of resources is my flaw
Someone once said;
'money is everything'
Maybe it's true
Money helps put life in order
Money helps meet life needs
Money is the ultimate measure of value
Yet still money doesn't buy us happiness
Yet still money doesn't buy us love and affection
Yet still money doesn't make our houses homely
