Street-Wize

Ponzi Schemes

There is word in the streets around the United States right now among many young adults. 'there is a very easy way of making extra money'. Who doesn’t want extra money, especially if it's passive income.

All you have to do is contribute $500 and invite two members to join. In 21 days you get paid $4000. if you want to stay, the circle continues, contribute and invite.

To a mathematical mind, this series is unsustainable. Where are the funds for pay-offs coming from? New members you are told. If you are lucky to have you two invites and get paid after 21 days, then this is the best thing in town!

I have a question, how many other two sets of successful invites need to be successful to raise a $4000 for you? Answer is 4! Yes four because eight sets of $500 makes $4000.

This means for you and your two invites to get paid again next 21 days you there is need for 24 more people to join and contribute their $500. This growth is exponential. Its like a pandemic. Eventually we come to a point where there is no new members joining either no-one can afford the $500 anymore or the whole population has joined.

What will happen to the last one who joined and contributed their $500s, who cannot invite anyone anymore because there is no one to invite?

The payouts crash on them. They can't get their refunds, they have paid off those who were due payoff. In this case every day is the 21st day, It means tomorrow there is people expecting payoff, but there is no money, and the day after until the situation explodes

This is called ponzi or pyramid scheme! Its fraud, it's been here as far back as the 1800s, why is it still successful? Maybe why are flue-like viruses still pandemic in 2020, they have been here since antiquity past, they had other names like plagues back then.
